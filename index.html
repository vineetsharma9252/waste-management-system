Frequently Asked Questions

What can I recycle?

You can recycle paper, glass, metal, and certain plastics. Check with your local recycling center for specific guidelines.

How can I start composting?

Begin by collecting organic waste such as food scraps and yard waste. Use a compost bin or pile and turn the material regularly.

What are some tips for reducing waste?

Reduce waste by buying in bulk, avoiding single-use items, and reusing products whenever possible. Implementing these practices can make a significant difference.
