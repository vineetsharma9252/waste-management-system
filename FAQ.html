Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this website?
This website is designed to help manage tasks related to garbage dumping and community hygiene.
How do I add a new task?
You can add a new task by navigating to the Schedule page and filling out the form with the task details.
Can I edit or delete tasks?
Currently, the feature to edit or delete tasks is not available. Please check back later for updates.
How can I contact support?
You can contact support by clicking on the Contact Us link at the bottom of the page.
